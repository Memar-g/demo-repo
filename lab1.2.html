Addis Ababa University

Addis Ababa University(Amharic:አዲስ አበባ ዩኒቨርሲቲ),or AAU , is an Ethiopian higher institute in Addis Ababa,Ethiopia.the main campus is located in the Arada subcity,6 kilo area.

Currently,AAU AAU has enrolled more than 50000 undergraduate (under regular and continuing education program) and close to 15000 postgraduate students in applied science, social science,Health science
and Enginnering and other fields, AAu is a university in the maiking, and much of its short-term plan aim at establishing academic infrastructures and facilities, staff requirment and manpower
development So far,we managed to recruit more than 15000 academic staff and administration staff.[1][2]
